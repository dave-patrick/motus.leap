Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-15

1. Use of the YouTube API Services

motus.leap uses the YouTube API Services. By using the Service, you acknowledge and agree that your use of the Service is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and the YouTube API Services Terms of Service. Through your Google account authorization, the Service accesses YouTube API data solely to provide playlist-management features.

2. Google Privacy Policy

Our handling of data obtained through Google's APIs is also described in the Google Privacy Policy. When you connect your account, Google's OAuth consent screen discloses the specific data requested.

3. Information We Access, Collect, Store, and Use

With your authorization, the Service accesses, collects, and stores the following YouTube API Data ("Authorized Data"):

  • Your YouTube channel and playlist metadata (titles, descriptions, IDs, thumbnails).
  • Playlist video items and their statistics (view counts, durations, publish dates) as Retrieved Data.
  • OAuth tokens required to act on your behalf for the features you enable.

We use this data only to display, organize, and manage your playlists within the Service, and to compute maintenance/duplicate reports you request. We do not use it for advertising or to train models.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information or Authorized Data. We share data only with:

  • Google's YouTube API, solely to perform the actions you request (e.g. moving or deleting videos in your playlists).
  • Our hosting/infrastructure providers, solely to operate the Service, under confidentiality obligations.

We do not share data with external advertising or analytics parties.

5. Third-Party Content and Advertising

The Service itself does not serve third-party advertisements. It displays YouTube thumbnail images and video metadata fetched from YouTube's own servers (e.g. i.ytimg.com), which are subject to YouTube's terms. Embedded or linked third-party content is governed by those third parties' policies.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use essential session cookies to keep you authenticated and to maintain your session state. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. You may clear or block cookies in your browser; doing so may require you to re-authenticate.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Cached statistics and playlist data retrieved via the YouTube API are stored on our servers for no more than 30 days, after which they are automatically deleted or refreshed. You may request deletion of your stored data at any time by emailing privacy@motus.leap.

To revoke the Service's access to your YouTube data at any time, visit your Google account's connected-apps settings: Google Security Settings — Connected apps. Revoking access immediately stops further API calls and triggers deletion of cached Authorized Data.

8. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to privacy@motus.leap.
